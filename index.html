About

Copy that knows what it’s doing.

Steve writes copy that is clear, readable and built around the audience it needs to reach. His work spans everything from websites and blog posts to press releases, social content and long-form documents.

With a background in journalism and years of freelance experience, he knows how to find the angle, shape the message and keep the writing sharp without overcomplicating it.

Background

More than just copywriting experience

Alongside commercial copywriting, Steve has worked in journalism and scriptwriting, giving him a strong instinct for structure, tone and audience. That background helps him write work that feels considered rather than filled out.

He has written across a wide range of subjects and sectors, adapting to different audiences without losing clarity. Whether the brief is commercial, editorial or research-led, the aim stays the same: write something worth reading.

“Good copy does not need to shout. It just needs to say the right thing, in the right way, to the right people.”
